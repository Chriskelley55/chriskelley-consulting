What gets audited

Every place time disappears.

We look at the full picture — not just your software, but how information moves through your business.

📥

Lead & inquiry handling

How new leads come in, who responds, how fast, and how many fall through the cracks.

🔄

Client onboarding

What happens after someone says yes — forms, contracts, kickoffs, access, and first steps.

📆

Scheduling & dispatch

How jobs get assigned, confirmed, updated, and followed up — especially for field-based teams.

💬

Communication patterns

Where communication lives (text, email, Slack, calls), what gets missed, and what repeats.

🧾

Invoicing & follow-up

How invoices go out, how late payments are handled, and how much time collections actually takes.

📊

Reporting & visibility

What you actually know about your business week-to-week vs. what requires digging to find out.

🛠️

Tools & tech stack

What software you're paying for, what's connected, what's duplicated, and what's being underused.

🔁

Recurring manual tasks

The stuff you do every week that feels like it should already be automatic — but isn't.

Example findings

What typically turns up.

Every audit is different, but these are the patterns that show up again and again.

HVAC Dispatch coordinator manually copying job details from email into scheduling software 15–20 times per day ~6 hrs/week saved
Roofing New leads from website sitting uncontacted for 4–6 hours while owner is on job sites 3× faster response
Healthcare Intake forms being re-entered manually into EHR — data that was already collected digitally ~8 hrs/week saved
Plumbing Invoice follow-up happening via individual text messages, no tracking, 30% of invoices going unpaid past 30 days +$4,200/mo recovered
Professional Services Client onboarding taking 3–5 days of back-and-forth for info that could be collected in a single form 1 day turnaround
