Digital intake that flows into your system

Patients fill out forms before they arrive. Data lands where it needs to — no re-entry, no paper chase at the front desk.

After-hours AI coverage

A chatbot handles "do you take my insurance?", "what are your hours?", and "I need to reschedule" at 10pm — so nothing falls through and no one needs to be on call.
