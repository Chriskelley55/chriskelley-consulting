What actually changes

These aren't complicated. They're just systems most service businesses haven't had time to put in place yet.

📲

Instant response when someone calls or texts after hours

Missed call triggers an automatic "Hey, got your message — here's how to reach us / book a time" reply. No leads lost to voicemail.

Tools: GoHighLevel · Zapier · Make · SMS trigger
📄

Estimate follow-up on autopilot

Quote goes out, follow-up texts go out day 2, day 5, day 10 — without you touching it. The jobs that were slipping through start closing.

Tools: HubSpot · GoHighLevel sequences
⭐

Review requests after every job

24 hours after completion, the customer gets a friendly text asking for a Google review. Most happy customers don't leave reviews because nobody asked. This fixes that.

Tools: NiceJob · Podium · Zapier → SMS
🧾

Invoicing and payment reminders that run themselves

Send, remind, reconcile — without the awkward "just checking in on that invoice" conversation. Automated reminders go out at 7, 14, and 30 days.

Tools: Jobber · HoneyBook · Wave + automation
🤝

Referral ask built into the follow-up

After a 5-star review, an automatic message asks if they know anyone who could use the same help. Referrals don't just happen — you have to ask. Now you always do.

Tools: GoHighLevel · ActiveCampaign · custom sequence
