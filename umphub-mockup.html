Buildout Overview
Slot Assignment: First-grab wins (default). Optional: admin-assign or invite-only per game. Rotisserie for season-long fairness on the back end.
Notifications: Push + SMS reminders 48hr before, slot-open alerts by level, payment due pings.
Venmo / Zelle: Deep-link to Venmo/Zelle app (free, no processing fees). Optional: Stripe for card payments if needed.
Ump Profiles: Level certs, availability calendar, ratings per game, season earnings history.
Admin Panel: Full schedule view, override assignments, ump roster management, season ledger export.
Stack: React Native (iOS + Android + web), Firebase/Supabase for real-time slots, Twilio for SMS, Expo for fast deploy.
