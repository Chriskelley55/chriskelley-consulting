The Process

How it works

Every engagement starts with understanding how your business actually runs — not how you think it runs. From there, we build and measure.

01

Map Your Week

Before we build anything, we need to see how you and your team actually spend your time. I use a lightweight activity tracking pipeline that runs quietly in the background — no timesheets, no manual logging, no extra steps for your team.

Screenpipe → n8n → Claude analysis
02

Find Where the Time Goes

The data gets automatically analyzed and turned into a clear scorecard: where your hours are going, which tasks are repetitive, and where the highest-leverage opportunities are hiding. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

Automated analysis + opportunity scoring
03

Prioritize What Moves the Needle

Not every automation is worth building. We rank opportunities by impact and effort — so we're always working on what gives you the most back, fastest. Quick wins first, bigger systems second.

04

Build the Systems

Automations, AI workflows, CRM connections, lead pipelines, AI agent employees — built to fit your actual tools and how your team works. Not generic templates, not duct-taped solutions.

n8n · Make · HubSpot · Claude Agents
05

Track the ROI

Every automation gets a number attached to it. Hours saved per month. Revenue influenced. Cost per lead reduced. We review it together monthly — so you always know exactly what you're getting.

Monthly ROI reporting included
